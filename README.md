## Project Overview

Brief history
TurboFix Automotive is a start-up vehicle repair and maintenance business focused on providing fast, reliable, and affordable automotive services. The business aims to serve individual car owners and small fleet operations by offering both routine maintenance and emergency repair solutions. The company is designed to operate with strong digital presence to attract modern customers who prefer booking services online.
Mission Statement:
TurboFix Automotive’s mission is to deliver high-quality automotive repair and maintenance services through a combination of skilled technicians, modern diagnostic tools, and customer-focused service delivery. The business aims to minimise vehicle downtime by transparent pricing. Additionally, TurboFix is committed to building long-term relationships with customers by ensuring trust reliability, and consistent service excellence.
Vision Statement:
The vision of TurboFix Automotive is to become a leading automotive service provider in South Africa, recognised for innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction. The company aims to expand its services through digital transformation, including online booking systems, service tracking, and customer communication platforms. In the long term, TurboFix seeks to grow into a multi-branch operation while maintaining high service standards.
Target Audience:
•	Individual car owners (ages 21-60)
•	Uber/Bolt drives
•	Small business fleet owner
•	Location: Urban areas, e.g., Pretoria or Johannesburg
